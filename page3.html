Belgian artist living in Ostend, working in Ghent. Originally from Armenia.

At a young age Meri started to develop a love for drawing and painting, influenced by her uncle who is an artist himself, she got inspired and started to create her own art.

In june 2016 she got accepted in the art school of Ghent, Luca School of Arts. That is also where she started her journey as a young painter.
